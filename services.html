Our Services

24-7 Around the Clock Monitoring

We are available to monitor and watch over
our residents at all hours of the day
in order to provide them with the best care possible.

Assistance with Daily Living Activities

Here at Freedom Estate, we enjoy providing
our residents with assistance in
all areas of life. This includes walking,
transferring, dressing, eating, bathing,
toileting, etc. We strive to be there for our
residents' every need, and tend to them as we
would our own family.

European and American-style Cuisines
(Including Snacks)

Our assisted living home provides complimentary
and customizable nutritional services for all our residents.
By catering to the personal needs of each resident,
we go just one step further in making everyone feel as though
they are truly at home at Freedom Estate.
We offer three delicious and healthy meals throughout
the day, as well as snacks in between.

Medical Reminders and Customized
Care Plans

Need to take medication or prescriptions daily?
Hourly? In between meals? In the morning or at night?
Need help to follow medical advice? Doctors orders?
Our staff will assist you through it all.
We will also customize a special care plan
just for you or your loved one.

Housekeeping and Laundry Services

Here at Freedom Estate, cleanliness is a top priority.
We believe that a clean home is a comfortable home.
Our staff maintains and conditions the home
to be clean and clutter free. We also
provide laundry service.

Assistance with Appointments
and Transportation

Monthly doctor visits? Hair appointments?
We will accommodate for transportation services to all
of our residents per request.

Fun Optional Daily Activities

We strive to keep our residents busy with fun
activities throughout the day, and will
do our best to make that possible. We are lucky
to have a young, practicing musician that is part of the
Freedom Estate family and can play piano upon request.

Therapy Dog

Meet our furry friend, Chloe. Chloe is
a calm, sweet poodle mix that loves to cuddle.
She does not shed, she is hypoallergenic, and
loves laying down and exploring the great outdoors. She is
not one to bark or lick, and is also potty trained.
