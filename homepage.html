Welcome to Our Home

Freedom Estate Assisted Living Home, located in Sterling, Alaska, offers a spectacular, modern living environment surrounded by the exquisite flora and fauna of the Alaskan territory. We offer individualized care for seniors as well as anyone eighteen years and older who experiences disabilities. We are dedicated to providing quality care that will give each resident the full satisfaction they deserve.

Why Freedom Estate?

We strive to make our beautiful house feel more like home everyday. No matter how cold Alaskan winters may be, at Freedom Estate we keep warm with the smiles of our residents. We work hard and make it our goal everyday to have the privilege to see our residents improve. The later years of life can be especially challenging, but Freedom Estate Assisted Living is here to help. At Freedom Estate, we listen to the needs of our residents in order to understand their limitations and discuss potential solutions.
